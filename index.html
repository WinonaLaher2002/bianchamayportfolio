I'm Biancha, a Bachelor of Science in Media and Communication graduate (July 2025) from Our Lady of Fatima University – Valenzuela. I specialize in creating fast-paced, engaging short-form videos that capture attention and communicate stories effectively.

My strength lies in producing scroll-stopping content for social media platforms, while continuously expanding my skills in cinematic and long-form storytelling. I enjoy experimenting with transitions, animations, and trend-forward edits that keep audiences hooked.

Open to both local Philippine and international clients — whether you need social media content, short film editing, or animation projects.
